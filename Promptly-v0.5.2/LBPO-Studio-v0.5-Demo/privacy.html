Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-31

1. Scope & Controller

This Privacy Policy explains how {PRODUCT_NAME} (“we”, “us”) handles information in the demo (static‑ui‑only) and cloud demo contexts. The demos are designed to minimize data collection and to keep information under your control. We do not run advertising or tracking scripts.

2. Data We Handle

  • Preference data: language selection, theme mode, cookie consent state.
  • Functional identifiers (optional): email and, if provided, name used to simulate sign‑up/login.
  • Subscription flags: Free vs Plus status used for UI display in demo flows.
  • Technical events: basic runtime states necessary to render charts locally; no analytics.

3. Purpose & Lawful Basis

We process preference data to render the UI in your chosen language and theme and to remember your choices when you consent. Functional identifiers and subscription flags are processed to simulate authentication and plan management during demonstrations. The legal basis under GDPR is your consent (Art. 6(1)(a)). You may withdraw consent at any time.

4. Storage & Retention

  • Static demo: data (preferences, simulated session flags) are stored only in your browser’s local storage; nothing is transmitted to us.
  • Cloud demo: minimal session objects may be held in server memory strictly for the duration of the demo; no persistent database is used.
  • Retention is user‑controlled: clear your browser data to delete local storage; ending your session clears in‑memory data server‑side.

5. Sharing & Transfers

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information. We do not transfer demo data to third countries or third‑party processors for analytics or advertising. If you choose to integrate external services on your own deployment, those services are subject to their providers’ policies.

6. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures suitable for a demo environment. Nevertheless, no system is perfectly secure. Do not submit sensitive personal data. Use anonymized test inputs whenever possible.

7. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may: (a) withdraw consent; (b) access and correct optional identifiers you entered; (c) delete locally stored data by clearing browser storage; (d) object to non‑essential processing (not applicable here because we avoid analytics). For assistance, contact privacy@example.com.

8. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or the age defined by your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect children’s data.

9. Changes

We may update this Policy; material updates will be reflected by the “Last updated” date. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

Questions: privacy@example.com.
