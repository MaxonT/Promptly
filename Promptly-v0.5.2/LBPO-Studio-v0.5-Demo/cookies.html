Cookie & Local Storage Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-31

1. Overview

This Policy describes how {PRODUCT_NAME} uses browser cookies and local storage in demo environments. We prioritize privacy and minimal data: we do not use third‑party analytics, advertising beacons, or cross‑site tracking identifiers. The app remains usable even if you reject non‑essential storage.

2. What We Store

  • consent: whether you accepted or rejected storage (value “1” or “0”).
  • lang: your selected interface language (e.g., “en”, “zh”).
  • theme: your chosen theme (“system”, “light”, “dark”).
  • user: optional sign‑in simulation object (email/name) when you choose “remember”.
  • plan: subscription flag for demo UI (e.g., “Free” or “Plus ($5/mo)”).

3. What We Do NOT Do

  • No advertising cookies, no fingerprinting, and no cross‑site identifiers.
  • No third‑party analytics libraries are loaded by default.
  • No sale or sharing of personal information.

4. Duration & Retention

Values in local storage persist until you clear them or revoke consent. Session‑only data (e.g., in‑memory flags in cloud demos) end with your session and are not written to a database.

5. Your Choices & Control

  • You can reject storage from the banner; defaults will be used each visit.
  • You can revoke consent at any time by clearing your browser’s local storage.
  • You can change language/theme without saving them; they will not persist without consent.

6. Regional Notes

For EU/EEA users, we rely on consent for non‑essential storage. For California residents, we do not sell or share personal information as defined by CCPA/CPRA. We honor user choice by design.

7. Changes

We may update this Policy; we will revise the “Last updated” date accordingly. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
